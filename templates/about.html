Welcome To Moonroc Studios

Background

Founded in 2018 by Civil Motha aka CivilTheSound, Moonroc Studios is a premier music haven nestled in the heart of Johannesburg, Fourways this cozy studio stands as a beacon of creativity and innovation in the South African music scene. . With a track record spanning documentaries, albums, singles, and beyond, our team, under the leadership of Civil Motha, has cultivated a reputation for excellence and ingenuity.

Vision

To be one of the leading creative spaces in Africa and to grow the South African music industry

Mission

To empower musicians, producers, and creators to unleash their full artistic potential
